The App is a party game that lets you add songs and players, randomly assign who sings what, and optionally import playlists from Spotify. It is designed to work primarily on your device, with minimal data sent to us. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. 1. Information We Collect We aim to keep data collection to the minimum necessary for the App to function and for you to send us feedback. We group information into two categories: 1.1. Data stored only on your device The following data is saved locally on your device (e.g. browser storage / local storage) and is not sent to our servers, except where explicitly stated: List of songs you add (title, artist, and whether it is Solo or Duet). List of players you add (player names and any songs you dedicate to them). Basic game state (e.g. random sequence of songs/players for your current game). This information is controlled by you and can usually be cleared by: Using in-app buttons such as “Clear Songs” or “Clear Players”, and/or Clearing your browser / app data or reinstalling the app. We do not have direct access to this local-only data. 1.2. Please review Spotify’s terms and privacy policy for more details. You can stop using the Spotify feature at any time by: Disconnecting within the App if available, and/or Revoking access to the App in your Spotify account settings. 3.
